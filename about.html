About NIFES Gidan Kwano

Christ-centered fellowship shaped for FUT Minna students

NIFES Gidan Kwano is the FUT Minna campus expression of the Nigerian Fellowship of Evangelical Students. We exist to form students who know Christ deeply, live with conviction, and serve their generation with grace, truth, and purpose.

Why NIFES exists

University life is a decisive season. NIFES helps students grow in faith, develop character, and learn to follow Jesus in practical ways while they study, lead, and prepare for life beyond campus.

About NIFES Minna

NIFES Minna is a Christ-centered fellowship of students committed to discipleship, evangelism, prayer, and loving community. Our local expression at Gidan Kwano is shaped by the same vision: to know God, love people, and make Christ known.

Formation story

Formed on the FUT Minna campus, NIFES Gidan Kwano began as a simple gathering of students hungry for God. What started as a prayerful response to the needs of campus life has grown into a fellowship where students encounter Scripture, build godly friendships, and learn to live on mission together.

What shapes us today

Our fellowship is shaped by Scripture, prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and service. We want every member to leave campus with a deeper walk with Christ and a clear sense of purpose.
