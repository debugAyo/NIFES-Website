Our Story

A Fellowship Shaped for Students

We exist to form students who know Christ deeply, live with conviction, and serve their generation with grace, truth, and purpose.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To raise a generation of Christ-centered leaders who will impact every sphere of Nigerian society with kingdom values, transforming campuses and communities.

Our Mission

To disciple students in the Word of God, foster authentic Christian community on campus, and equip them to be salt and light in their spheres of influence.

Our History

Humble Beginnings

Formed on the FUT Minna campus, NIFES Gidan Kwano began as a simple gathering of students hungry for God. What started as a prayerful response to the needs of campus life has grown into a fellowship where students encounter Scripture, build godly friendships, and learn to live on mission together.

Growth & Impact

Over the years, the fellowship expanded through intentional discipleship and consistent outreach. Today, NIFES Gidan Kwano continues to strengthen its campus witness through a deep commitment to Scripture, prayer, and service, impacting countless lives along the way.

What Shapes Us

Our fellowship is shaped by these core biblical values.

The Word of God

We believe Scripture is the ultimate authority for life and faith. Everything we do is centered on knowing and obeying God's Word.

Prayer & Worship

We are a community dependent on God, expressing our love and reliance on Him through consistent prayer and heartfelt worship.

Discipleship

We are committed to helping students grow into mature followers of Christ through mentoring, small groups, and accountability.

Community

We foster a loving and supportive family where students can build authentic relationships and experience genuine Christian fellowship.

Evangelism & Mission

We are passionate about sharing the gospel on campus and beyond, equipping students to be effective witnesses for Christ.

Leadership Development

We intentionally raise and equip students to become servant-leaders who will make a godly impact in the church and society.

NIFES.

Raising Christ-like students to transform the campus and society.
