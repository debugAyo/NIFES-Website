Our weekly rhythm

We gather on Sunday to worship, on Tuesday to pray, and on Thursday to study Scripture together. Each meeting is designed to help students grow spiritually and live with purpose on campus.

What to expect

Expect worship, Bible-centered teaching, open fellowship, and a warm community where students can belong, learn, and serve.
