Fellowship Life

Our Weekly Gatherings

Intentional times for worship, prayer, and growing in the Word together.

Our Weekly Rhythm

Sunday Service

The cornerstone of our week. A time of corporate worship, powerful preaching from the Word, and fellowship as one body in Christ. The perfect way to re-center your heart for the week ahead.

Time: 4:00 PM

Tuesday Prayer Meeting

Join us as we seek God's face together. A dedicated time to intercede for our campus, our nation, and one another, strengthening our dependence on God.

Time: 7:00 PM

Thursday Bible Study

Dive deeper into God's Word in a more interactive, small group setting. It's a safe place to ask questions, share insights, and grow in your understanding of Scripture.

Time: 7:00 PM

Special Events

Beyond our weekly meetings, we host special events throughout the semester.

Conferences & Retreats

Semester-based events focused on spiritual renewal, in-depth teaching, and extended times of fellowship away from the pressures of academic life.

Outreach & Evangelism

Organized events to share the love of Christ with the campus community through service, open-air meetings, and personal evangelism.

Social Gatherings

Fun and relaxed events like movie nights, sports days, and picnics designed to build stronger relationships within the fellowship family.
